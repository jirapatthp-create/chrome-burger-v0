About Us

Chrome & Burger is not just a food truck; it's a culinary journey on wheels. Born from a passion for amazing food and classic American style, we bring you the juiciest, most flavorful burgers you've ever tasted. Our secret? We use only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients and cook every burger to perfection.

Our truck is a gleaming chrome beauty, a nod to the classic diners of the past, but our food is modern, creative, and always delicious. We're more than just a meal; we're an experience. Find us on the streets and taste the difference.
